Concept, Genre, and References


The Waters of Bloomshazy is a game where your fish tries to survive pollution
in the wild which we took inspriation from real life fish deaths. I would say that it is an endless sidescroller for its genre. It was inspired by mobile games with no end
and in more specific example Jetpack joyride.
High Concept: maneuver fish to survive trash.
